What are comics?

    A comic is a hand-drawn story, a story in pictures. The comic combines the features of such art forms as literature and fine art. A kind of book and magazine illustration, sometimes used in print media. It is not a literary genre. There are many different names for this kind of art and its individual varieties. Large-scale comics with a cross-cutting plot are called graphic novels.
    History comics
    The appearance of modern comics was preceded by the political cartoons of the XVIII century by William Hogarth.They were a series of drawings united by a common thought.
    The comic became not just a fun set of drawings with text, it began to reflect and reflect the events taking place in the world. By the beginning of the XX century, every newspaper had a strip with comics, which reflected various public issues.
